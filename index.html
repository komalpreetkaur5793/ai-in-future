What Exactly is AI?

Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by computer systems. These processes include learning (acquiring information and rules), reasoning (using rules to reach conclusions), and self-correction.

From virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa to recommendation engines on Netflix and Amazon, AI is already deeply embedded in our daily lives. It encompasses machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics.

Machine Learning
Algorithms that learn from data without being explicitly programmed
Natural Language Processing
Enabling machines to understand and respond in human language
Computer Vision
Training machines to interpret and understand visual information
Robotics & Automation
Physical machines powered by AI to perform complex tasks autonomously
